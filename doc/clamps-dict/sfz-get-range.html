sfz-get-range

Function 

(sfz-get-range ref)

Return the keynum range of sfz denoted by ref.

Arguments:

ref Reference to the sfz. Can be the keyword or symbol of a registered sfz preset or a filename (pathname or string) of a sfz file. Relative filenames will be searched recursively in ∗sfz-file-path∗.
